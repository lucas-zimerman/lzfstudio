Clients

We are proud to have Sentry.io as our main client. We actively contribute to maintaining and enhancing their SDKs, ensuring top-notch performance and reliability for their users.

Sentry is an application monitoring and error tracking platform with more than 90.000 teams that helps developers identify, diagnose, and fix issues in their software applications. It provides real-time insights into errors, crashes, and performance issues, allowing teams to prioritize and resolve issues efficiently. With features like error aggregation, issue alerts, and detailed error reports, Sentry empowers developers to deliver high-quality software and improve user experience.
